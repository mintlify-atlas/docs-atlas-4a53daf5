## Platform Overview

Safaris Web is built on Laravel and provides:

- **Tour Discovery**: Dynamic category and subcategory filtering via `/tours/{category}` and `/tours/categories/{sub_category}` routes
- **Search Functionality**: Advanced search to find safaris by destination, hotel, or preferences (route: `/search`)
- **User Profiles**: Manage your bookings and view history at `/profile`
- **Content Library**: Access travel blogs and video content to inspire your journey
- **Multiple Services**: Tours, accommodations, events, and additional services all in one platform

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up with email verification to access booking features. Route: `/register`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse & Search">
    Explore safaris by category or use search to find specific destinations at `/safaris`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Safari">
    View detailed tour information including hotels, transport, meals, and pricing per person sharing or single room
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Booking">
    Provide traveler details, arrival date, and transaction code via `/bookings/create/{destination}`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Receive booking confirmation within 24 hours and manage your booking from your profile
  </Step>
</Steps>

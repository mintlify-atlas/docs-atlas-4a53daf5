Get up and running with Safaris Web quickly. This guide walks you through creating an account, browsing tours, and making your first booking.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit the Safaris Web homepage and register for an account.

    **Required Information:**
    - Full name (first and last name)
    - Email address
    - Password (must be secure)
    - Phone number

    <Note>
      After registration, you'll receive a verification email. You must verify your email address before making bookings.
    </Note>

    **Email Verification Process:**

    1. Check your email inbox for a verification link
    2. Click the verification link to activate your account
    3. You'll be redirected to the homepage once verified

    If you don't receive the email:
    - Check your spam/junk folder
    - Click "Resend verification email" from the verification notice page
    - The system allows up to 6 verification emails per hour (throttled)

    <Info>
      Email verification is required to make bookings. This ensures we can send you booking confirmations and important updates.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse available tours">
    Explore the safari and tour offerings through multiple discovery methods.

    **Browse by Category:**

    Navigate to `/safaris` to view all available tours, or browse by specific categories:

    - **Safari tours** - Wildlife safaris, game drives, national park visits
    - **Adventure tours** - Hiking, mountain climbing, water sports
    - **Cultural experiences** - Village visits, cultural immersion, heritage sites
    - **Beach holidays** - Coastal resorts, water activities, island getaways
    - **Specialty tours** - Photography safaris, bird watching, conservation tours

    **Tour Information Displayed:**

    Each tour listing shows:
    - Tour name and destination
    - Featured image (main thumbnail)
    - Pricing options (per person sharing / single room)
    - Tour category
    - Brief description
    - Duration and itinerary highlights

    **View Detailed Tour Information:**

    Click on any tour to see:
    - Complete itinerary and schedule
    - Full pricing breakdown
    - Accommodation details (hotel/lodge information)
    - Transportation arrangements
    - Meals included
    - Up to 6 high-quality images
    - What's included and excluded
    - Booking requirements

    <Tip>
      Tours are paginated with 20 tours per page. Use the pagination controls at the bottom to browse through all available options.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make your first booking">
    Once you've found the perfect tour, submit a booking request.

    **Booking Process:**

    1. **From the tour details page**, click the "Book Now" button
    2. You'll be directed to `/bookings/create/{destination}` where `{destination}` is the tour name
    3. Complete the booking form with the following information:

    **Required Booking Information:**

    ```javascript
    {
      first_name: "Your first name",
      last_name: "Your last name",
      email: "your.email@example.com",
      phone_number: "Your contact number",
      adults: "Number of adult travelers",
      kids: "Number of children",
      destination: "Tour/safari destination",
      check_in: "Check-in date",
      check_out: "Check-out date",
      amount: "Total booking amount",
      status: "pending", // Automatically set
      transaction_code: "Generated unique code"
    }
    ```

    <Note>
      You must be logged in and have a verified email address to submit bookings. The form will redirect to login if you're not authenticated.
    </Note>

    **Security & Validation:**

    - **reCAPTCHA Protection**: All booking submissions are protected by Google reCAPTCHA v3 to prevent spam and automated abuse
    - **Authentication Required**: The booking submission route requires both `auth` and `verified` middleware
    - **Form Validation**: Server-side validation ensures all required fields are provided

    **After Submission:**

    1. Your booking is created with status `pending`
    2. A unique transaction code is generated for tracking
    3. You'll be redirected to your bookings dashboard at `/bookings`
    4. You can view your booking details at `/bookings/show/{id}`

    <Warning>
      Bookings are initially marked as "pending" and require admin confirmation. You'll receive updates once your booking is reviewed.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage your bookings">
    Track and manage all your safari bookings from your personal dashboard.

    **Access Your Bookings:**

    Navigate to `/bookings` (requires login and verified email) to see:

    - All your booking requests
    - Booking status (pending, confirmed, cancelled)
    - Tour details and dates
    - Transaction codes
    - Total amounts

    **Booking Actions:**

    - **View Details**: Click on any booking to see full information
    - **Edit Booking**: Modify dates, traveler count, or other details (route: `/bookings/edit/{id}`)
    - **Cancel Booking**: Request cancellation for pending bookings

    **Profile Management:**

    Access your profile at `/profile` to:
    - Update your personal information
    - View booking history
    - Manage account settings
    - Change password

    <Info>
      Your booking history is permanently saved and accessible anytime. Completed tours remain in your history for future reference.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Questions

<Accordion title="Do I need to verify my email to browse tours?">
  No, you can browse all tours without logging in. However, email verification is required to make bookings or submit enquiries.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does booking confirmation take?">
  Booking confirmations are processed by administrators. You'll receive an email notification once your booking status changes from "pending" to "confirmed". Check the admin team's working hours for typical response times.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I modify a booking after submission?">
  Yes, you can edit pending bookings through your bookings dashboard at `/bookings/edit/{id}`. Once a booking is confirmed, contact the admin team for modifications.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
  Payment details and methods are confirmed after your booking request is reviewed by the admin team. You'll receive payment instructions via email.
</Accordion>

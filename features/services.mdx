## Overview

Safaris Web offers additional services beyond tours, accommodations, and events. The Services feature provides individual, bookable services that enhance your Kenya travel experience.

<Info>
  Services are managed through the `Service` model (`app/Models/Service.php`) and stored in the `services` database table.
</Info>

## Service Structure

From the `create_services_table` migration:

### Database Schema

<Tabs>

## Browsing Services

### Main Services Page

**Route:** `/services`

**Controller:** `ServiceController::index()`

**Display Logic:**
```php

```

**Features:**
- All published services displayed
- Ordered by most recent first
- Category filters available
- Image previews for each service

## Admin Management Routes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="View All">
    **Admin View:**
    - Route: `/all-services`
    - Controller: `ServiceController::all()`
    - Middleware: `auth`, `verified`
    - Shows all services including unpublished
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Create">
    **New Service:**
    - Form: `/service/new` (GET)
    - Submit: `/service/create` (POST)
    - Middleware: `auth`, `verified`
    - Requires: name, category, location, description, price, 2 images
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Edit">
    **Update Service:**
    - Form: `/service/edit/{id}` (GET)
    - Update: `/service/update/{id}` (PUT)
    - Middleware: `auth`, `verified`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Publish">
    **Toggle Status:**
    - Route: `/service/publish/{id}` (PUT)
    - Toggles `published` between 0 and 1
    - Middleware: `auth`, `verified`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Delete">
    **Remove Service:**
    - Route: `/service/delete/{id}` (DELETE)
    - Permanently removes service
    - Middleware: `auth`, `verified`
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  All admin routes require authentication and email verification. Regular users cannot create or manage services.
</Warning>

## Services vs Other Features

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="vs Tours">
    **Services:**
    - Individual, modular offerings
    - Add to existing plans
    - Flexible scheduling
    - Service-specific pricing
    
    **Tours:**
    - Complete packages
    - Fixed itineraries
    - Multi-day experiences
    - All-inclusive pricing
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="vs Accommodations">
    **Services:**
    - Activities and support
    - Time-based or per-service
    - Enhance travel experience
    
    **Accommodations:**
    - Lodging focused
    - Per night pricing
    - Place to stay
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="vs Events">
    **Services:**
    - Available on-demand (subject to booking)
    - Flexible timing
    - Support services
    
    **Events:**
    - Fixed dates and times
    - Scheduled experiences
    - Event-focused
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="vs Products">
    **Services:**
    - Service-based (doing)
    - Time or experience based
    - Often requires provider presence
    
    **Products:**
    - Goods-based (physical items)
    - Purchase and own
    - One-time transaction
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Publishing Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Service">
    Admin creates service with all details and 2 images
  </Step>
  <Step title="Draft Status">
    Initially `published = 0` (not visible to public)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    Verify pricing, description, availability, images
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Set `published = 1` via publish route
  </Step>
  <Step title="Live">
    Service appears on `/services` page and homepage
  </Step>
</Steps>

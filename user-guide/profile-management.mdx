## Overview

Your profile is your central hub for managing bookings, viewing history, and maintaining your account information on Safaris Web. This guide covers all profile features and management capabilities.

<Info>
  **Profile Access:** `/profile` route requires authentication and email verification (`auth` and `verified` middleware).
</Info>

## Accessing Your Profile

**Route:** `/profile`

**Controller:** `BookingController::profile()`

**Requirements:**
- Must be logged in
- Email must be verified
- Active session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login">
    Access the login page at `/login` and enter your credentials
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile">
    Click on your profile link or navigate directly to `/profile`
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Dashboard">
    Your profile dashboard displays all your bookings and account information
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Dashboard

### What's Displayed

Your profile shows:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Booking History" icon="clock-rotate-left">

  </Card>

```php
Booking::where('user_id', Auth::user()->id)->latest()->paginate(10)
```

This ensures:
- Only your bookings are displayed (filtered by `user_id`)
- Latest bookings appear first (`latest()` ordering)
- Manageable pagination (10 per page)

## Booking History

### Viewing Your Bookings

Each booking in your history displays:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Traveler Information">
    - **Name:** `first_name` + `last_name`
    - **Email:** Contact email address
    - **Phone:** `phone_number`
    - **Country:** Your country of residence
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Travel Details">
    - **Destination:** Safari/tour name
    - **Arrival Date:** When your safari starts
    - **Adults:** Number of adult travelers
    - **Kids:** Number of children
    - **Special Requirements:** Any special needs noted
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Booking Info">
    - **Transaction Code:** Payment reference
    - **Status:** 0 (pending) or 1 (confirmed)
    - **Booking ID:** Unique identifier
    - **Created:** Timestamp when booking was made
    - **Updated:** Last modification timestamp
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Understanding Booking Status

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Status: 0 (Pending)">
    **What it means:**
    - Booking submitted successfully
    - Awaiting admin review and confirmation
    - You should be contacted within 24 hours
    
    **What you can do:**
    - Edit booking details if needed
    - Cancel if plans change
    - Wait for confirmation communication
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Status: 1 (Confirmed)">
    **What it means:**
    - Admin has reviewed and confirmed your booking
    - Payment verified
    - Safari is confirmed and scheduled
    
    **What you can do:**
    - View final confirmed details
    - Contact support for any changes (editing may be restricted)
    - Prepare for your safari
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  Status is updated by admins through the `/bookings/confirm/{id}` route which toggles between 0 and 1.
</Info>

## Managing Individual Bookings

### Viewing Booking Details

**Route:** `/bookings/show/{id}`

**Middleware:** `auth`, `verified`

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Booking">
    From your profile, click on any booking to view full details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Information">
    The detail page shows all booking data:
    - Complete traveler information
    - Full travel details
    - Transaction code
    - Special requirements
    - Booking timestamps
    - Current status
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Controller:** `BookingController::show(Booking $booking)`

### Editing Bookings

<Warning>
  You can only edit pending bookings (status = 0). Confirmed bookings may require admin assistance to modify.
</Warning>

**Route:** `/bookings/edit/{id}`

**Middleware:** `auth`, `verified`

**Editable Fields:**

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Personal Info">
    - First name (required)
    - Last name (required)
    - Email (required)
    - Phone number (required)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Travel Info">
    - Arrival date (required)
    - Number of adults (required)
    - Number of kids (required)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

**Update Process:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Edit Form">
    Click edit on the booking from your profile or navigate to `/bookings/edit/{id}`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Modify Fields">
    Update any information that needs changes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Changes">
    POST to `/bookings/update/{id}` with updated data
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Success message: "Booking updated successfully!"
    
    Redirects to the bookings admin page
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Validation Rules:**
```php
$request->validate([
    'first_name' => 'required',
    'last_name' => 'required',
    'email' => 'required',
    'phone_number' => 'required',
    'arrival_date' => 'required',
    'adults' => 'required',
    'kids' => 'required',
]);
```

### Canceling Bookings

**Route:** `/bookings/delete/{id}` (DELETE method)

**Middleware:** `auth`, `verified`

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Booking">
    From your profile, choose the booking you want to cancel
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Click the cancel/delete button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Booking Removed">
    The booking is permanently deleted from the database
    
    Success message: "Booking deleted successfully!"
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Controller:** `BookingController::destroy(Booking $booking, $id)`

<Warning>
  Deletion is permanent. The booking record is removed from the `tour_bookings` table and cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  If you need to cancel a confirmed booking, consider contacting support through `/enquiries` instead of deleting, as you may need records for refund processing.
</Tip>

## Account Information

### User Model Data

Your account is managed through the `User` model (`app/Models/User.php`):

**Stored Information:**
- `name` - Your full name
- `email` - Email address (must be verified)
- `email_verified_at` - Email verification timestamp
- `password` - Securely hashed password
- `remember_token` - For "Remember Me" functionality
- `created_at` - Account creation date
- `updated_at` - Last account update

**User-Booking Relationship:**
```php
public function bookings()
{
    return $this->hasMany('App\Booking');
}
```

This establishes that one user can have many bookings, all accessible through your profile.

## Email Verification Status

Your profile access depends on email verification:

### Checking Verification

**Route:** `/email/verify`

**What You See:**
- If verified: Full access to profile and booking features
- If not verified: Verification notice with option to resend link

### Resending Verification Email

**Route:** `/email/verification-notification` (POST)

**Middleware:** `auth`, throttled to 6 requests per minute

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request New Link">
    If you didn't receive the original email, click "Resend verification link"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    Look for email with verification link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Link">
    Link format: `/email/verify/{id}/{hash}` with signed middleware
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-Redirect">
    Upon verification, automatically redirected to home page
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Message after resending: "Verification link sent!"
</Info>

## Pagination Controls

If you have more than 10 bookings:

- **Page Parameter:** `?page=2`, `?page=3`, etc.
- **Items Per Page:** 10 bookings
- **Ordering:** Latest bookings first
- **Index Variable:** `(request()->input('page', 1) - 1) * 5` for proper numbering

<Tip>
  Use pagination controls at the bottom of your profile to navigate through older bookings.
</Tip>

## Security Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="User ID Filtering">
    All booking queries filter by your `user_id`:
    ```php
    where('user_id', Auth::user()->id)
    ```
    This ensures you only see your own bookings, never other users' data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Authentication Required">
    Profile routes use `auth` middleware. You must be logged in with valid session to access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Email Verification Required">
    Profile routes use `verified` middleware. Your email must be verified to access booking features.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="CSRF Protection">
    All forms (edit, delete) include CSRF tokens to prevent cross-site request forgery attacks.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't Access Profile">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Not logged in → Login at `/login`
    - Email not verified → Check email for verification link or resend at `/email/verification-notification`
    - Session expired → Login again
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Don't See My Bookings">
    **Check:**
    - Logged in with the correct account (bookings are user-specific)
    - Booking was successfully submitted (check for confirmation message)
    - Page number if you have many bookings (pagination)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't Edit Booking">
    **Reasons:**
    - Booking is already confirmed (status = 1) → Contact support
    - Not logged in or session expired → Login again
    - Trying to edit someone else's booking → Only your bookings are editable
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Profile Loads Slowly">
    **If you have many bookings:**
    - System loads 10 at a time (pagination)
    - Check your internet connection
    - Large number of bookings loads latest first for better performance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
